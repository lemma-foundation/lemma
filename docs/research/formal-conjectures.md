# Formal Conjectures

Google DeepMind Formal Conjectures is a public collection of formal mathematical statements.

Lemma references it as a frontier benchmark and downstream demonstration layer. Lemma does not primarily pay miners to solve Formal Conjectures tasks, does not custody funds for those statements, and is not endorsed by Google DeepMind.

The intended relationship is narrow: Formal Conjectures can be used as held-out
benchmark context or downstream demonstration material. It is not the paid task
source, not an endorsement, and not a custody or payout surface.

Paid training tasks and held-out benchmark claims must stay separate. A task used as public benchmark evidence should not also be paid as ordinary training work.
